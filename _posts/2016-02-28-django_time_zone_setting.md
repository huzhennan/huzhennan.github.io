#Django设置TIME_ZONE和LANGUAGE_CODE为中国区域

设置为中国区域：
LANGUAGE_CODE = 'zh-hans'

TIME_ZONE = 'Asia/Shanghai'

**注：**可以在[site-packages\django\conf\global_settings.py](site-packages\django\conf\global_settings.py)
查到详细的设置
